Meet Your Doctor

Dr Mushtaq MD is a board-certified adult psychiatrist with a fellowship in child and adolescent psychiatry and over 17 years of experience. She was trained at New York Medical College and has been practicing in the Chicagoland area since 2012.

She has a firm belief in a holistic approach to psychiatry beyond medication - partnering with families, schools, and state agencies to create supportive environments that help patients thrive while staying aligned with the latest advances in psychiatric care.

Her clinical focus includes mood and anxiety disorders, trauma and psychotic conditions, developmental and autism spectrum disorders, perinatal mental health, and behavioral challenges across the lifespan.
