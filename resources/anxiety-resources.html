Anxiety Disorders Resources

Educational information about anxiety disorders, panic attacks, stress responses, and evidence-based treatment options.

Understanding Anxiety Disorders

Anxiety disorders are among the most common mental health conditions and can affect emotional wellbeing, physical health, relationships, work performance, and daily functioning.

While occasional stress is a normal part of life, persistent anxiety that interferes with functioning may benefit from professional support.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent worry or racing thoughts
  • Panic attacks or sudden intense fear
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Muscle tension or physical stress symptoms
  • Avoidance behaviors
  • Difficulty concentrating

Treatment Approaches

Anxiety disorders are highly treatable. Treatment may include psychotherapy, medication management, lifestyle interventions, sleep optimization, mindfulness strategies, and stress reduction.

Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) is one of the most researched and effective approaches for anxiety-related conditions.

Trusted Organizations

  • NIMH Anxiety Disorders
  • Anxiety & Depression Association
  • SAMHSA

When To Seek Help

Consider professional evaluation if anxiety symptoms are causing significant distress, interfering with work or relationships, affecting sleep, or limiting daily activities.
