Mental Health Resource Library

Trusted educational resources, treatment information, and support tools for patients and families navigating mental health care.

Need immediate help?

If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or needs urgent emotional support, these services are available 24/7.

Call or Text 988 Immediate crisis support for emotional distress, suicidal thoughts, or mental health emergencies. Crisis Text Support Free text-based crisis counseling with trained responders available anytime. Illinois CARES Statewide mental health and recovery resources for Illinois residents.
